# Strategic Governance & AI Risk Framework (GRC)
### *Operationalizing Compliance for Global Identity & AI Systems*

This repository demonstrates the intersection of **Cybersecurity GRC** and **AI Regulation**. It provides an operational blueprint for managing digital risk in high-stakes environments, specifically mapping the **EU AI Act** and **ISO 27001** standards to real-world workflows.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- **Regulatory Alignment:** Translating the EU AI Act into actionable Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
- **Risk Mitigation:** Designing Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) protocols for automated identity verification.
- **Security Governance:** Integrating ISO 27001 controls within AI-driven operational lifecycles.
